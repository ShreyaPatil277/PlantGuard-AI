About PlantGuard AI

PlantGuard AI is an advanced plant disease detection system powered by deep learning and computer vision. Our model is trained on over 87,900 images across 38 disease classes, achieving 96-99% accuracy.


Technology: MobileNetV2 Transfer Learning, TensorFlow, Flask

Dataset: New Plant Diseases Dataset (PlantVillage)

Purpose: Helping farmers and gardeners detect plant diseases early for better crop management.
